Ramen Noodles

Learn to make ramen noodles that beat out store bought every time!

Follow along to find out how!

    Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 Cups vegetable broth
  • 3.5 Ounce package ramen noodles with dried vegetables
  • 2 Teaspoons of soy sauce
  • A half teaspoon of chili oil
  • A half teaspoon of minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • 2 green onions (sliced)

    Steps

    How to Make Ramen Noodles Step-By-Step

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine broth and noodles
  2. Cover and bring to a boil over high heat
  3. Stir to break up noodles
  4. Reduce heat to medium and add soy sauce, chili oil, and ginger
  5. Simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes
  6. Stir in sesame oil and garnish with green onions
  7. Let rest for 3 minutes as contents are VERY HOT

    8. ENJOY!
